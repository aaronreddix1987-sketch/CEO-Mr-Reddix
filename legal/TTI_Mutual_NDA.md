# MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of April 15, 2026, between Total Transformation Inc. ("TTI"), located at 877 E 43rd Pl, Los Angeles CA 90011, and the counterparty.

## 1. Definition of Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" means any proprietary information, technical data, trade secrets, or know-how, including, but not limited to, research, product plans, products, services, customer lists, software, developments, inventions, processes, formulas, technology, designs, drawings, engineering, hardware configuration information, marketing, finances, or other business information disclosed by either party.

## 2. Obligations
Each party agrees to hold the other party's Confidential Information in strict confidence and not to disclose such Confidential Information to any third parties without the prior written consent of the disclosing party.

## 3. Term
This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of five (5) years from the date of disclosure.
