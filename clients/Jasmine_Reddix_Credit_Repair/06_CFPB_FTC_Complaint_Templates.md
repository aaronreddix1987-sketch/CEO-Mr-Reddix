# CFPB & FTC Official Complaint Templates
**Client:** Jasmine U. Reddix
**Date:** April 2, 2026

## CFPB Complaint Template
**To be filed at:** consumerfinance.gov/complaint

**Subject:** Credit Bureaus Failing to Verify Debt Under FCRA Sec 609/611

**Description:**
I have submitted formal dispute letters to Experian, Equifax, and TransUnion requesting verification of negative items on my credit report under FCRA Sections 609 and 611. The bureaus have failed to provide the original signed contracts from the original creditors proving my liability, yet they refuse to delete the unverified items. This is a direct violation of the Fair Credit Reporting Act. I am requesting the CFPB intervene and force the bureaus to either produce the original signed documentation or delete the unverified tradelines immediately.

## FTC Complaint Template
**To be filed at:** reportfraud.ftc.gov

**Subject:** FCRA Violation - Unverified Credit Reporting

**Description:**
Experian, Equifax, and TransUnion are reporting inaccurate and unverified negative information on my consumer credit file. Despite my formal written requests under FCRA Section 611 for reinvestigation and Section 609 for original documentation, the bureaus continue to report these items without proper verification. This is causing me financial harm and violates federal law. I request an FTC investigation into their reporting practices regarding my file.
